You may not know how, or you may not think your help is significant enough to really make a difference.

You are wrong.

There are many ways you can help that will actually make a difference and help the environment around you. In order to reduce gasses released into the air, you can carpool, ride a bike, or ride the bus. This also reduces gas consumption and is generally cheaper.

You can also:

  • Reduce the level of fossil fuel use in your daily life, this causes more greenhouse gases to get released into the atmosphere and therefore causing global warming
  • Plant trees, it released carbon dioxide which is the most important greenhouse gas and it can actually help stop global warming.
  • Conserve water, it supplies lots of the energy that goes to cities and that helps contribute to greenhouse gas emissions
  • Reduce the amount of waste, the production of garbage is a big contribution to global warming
